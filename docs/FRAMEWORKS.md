# Frameworks to Build

Skills to create, prioritized by PM usefulness. Each skill teaches the framework + when/how to apply it.

| Framework | When to Use It | Status | Patterns |
|-----------|----------------|--------|----------|
| Jobs-to-be-Done (JTBD) | Understanding why customers hire/fire products, discovering unmet needs | ✅ | 14 |
| PMF Survey | Measuring product-market fit, deciding whether to pivot or double down | ✅ | 15 |
| Positioning Canvas | Launching a product, entering new markets, repositioning against competitors | ✅ | 14 |
| 7 Powers | Evaluating competitive moats, strategic planning, assessing business durability | ✅ | 14 |
| Growth Loops | Designing sustainable acquisition/retention engines, moving beyond one-off tactics | ✅ | 15 |
| Working Backwards | Scoping new features or products, writing PRDs, aligning teams on vision | ✅ | 14 |
| Opportunity Solution Trees | Prioritizing discovery work, connecting research to outcomes | ✅ | 14 |
| Shape Up | Planning work in cycles, shipping without endless sprints, scoping projects | ✅ | 14 |
| Monetizing Innovation | Setting pricing, designing packaging, validating willingness-to-pay | ✅ | 14 |
| Hierarchy of Engagement | Designing retention mechanics, identifying core actions, building habit loops | ✅ | 13 |
| Trustworthy Experiments | Running valid A/B tests, interpreting results, building experiment culture | ✅ | 14 |
| Strategic Narrative | Pitching to customers/investors, aligning sales messaging, company positioning | ✅ | 14 |
| Hooked Model | Designing habit-forming features, increasing engagement, building triggers | ✅ | 17 |
| Thinking in Bets | Making decisions under uncertainty, avoiding cognitive biases, probabilistic thinking | ✅ | 14 |
| Radical Candor | Giving feedback, managing people, having difficult conversations | ✅ | 14 |
| Product-Led Growth | Deciding self-serve vs sales-led, designing freemium, optimizing conversion | ✅ | 15 |
| OKRs | Setting goals, aligning teams, measuring progress quarterly | ✅ | 14 |
| Design Sprint | Validating ideas quickly, prototyping before building, team alignment | ✅ | 14 |
| Hierarchy of Marketplaces | Building marketplace liquidity, solving chicken-and-egg, scaling supply/demand | ✅ | 15 |
| Product-Led SEO | Building organic acquisition, creating programmatic pages, content strategy | ✅ | 14 |
